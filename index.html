Experience Climbing Dorset

At Southside Climbing we want to share the joy and adventure of climbing in the south coast. Our sessions are for range from beginners to experienced climbers. You'll also get to take in the stunning views of Dorset and it's wildlife!

Our sessions

We run a variety of different sessions in Dorset. Whether you are looking to try something new or you want an action packed holiday, there's something for you.

About Me

"Hello, I'm Hayden, the owner of Southside Climbing. I've been climbing since I was 15, since then it's become a huge part of my life. I love climbing because it challenges me physically and mentally. There's always a climb that I'm challenging myself on, once I've climbed something I've worked, on I get a real sense of achievement. Climbing is for everyone, I'm proud to be part of such a friendly and diverse communitity."
