Southside Climbing

Experience Climbing Dorset

At Southside Climbing we want to share the joy and adventure of climbing in the south coast. Our sessions are for beginners from ages 9+, ideal for families that would like to try something new.

What's included

We provide helmets and harnesses to ensure your safety. We can also teach you how to belay and tie climbing knots.

Bookings

We run 3 hour sessions in small groups. You can view our prices and book through Airbnb. For private booking, please contact us on the form below.
