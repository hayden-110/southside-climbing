Learn to Belay

In this session you’ll learn how to climb and belay each other on a rope. With these skills you’ll be able to climb at your local roped wall on bottom ropes and progress on to sport climbing. Here in Dorset, our coast is full of stunning places to climb. We provide all the necessary safety equipment and our expertise of rock climbing.

Private Guiding

8 Hours · 2-6 Climbers · Ages 7+

On booking, you will have the choice of either climbing at Portland or Swanage. Each of these locations have multiple climbing spots, we like to choose where the best conditions are to climb, but if you have a preference of crag, just let us know.

Open Sessions

8 Hours · Upto 6 Climbers · Ages 12+

These sessions are great for meeting new people who also want to start climbing. We run our open sessions on the last Sunday of every month at Portland. There are 6 spaces on each session, no more than 2 climbers when booking.

All Equipment Included

You'll Need:

  • A sturdy pair of shoes
  • Lunch, snacks & water
  • A jumper and waterproof

Where will I meet my Instructor?

We will send you an exact location once we receive a good forecast. We’ll get in touch a couple of days before your session.

Can I bring my dog?

Your welcome to bring your dog on our private session as long as you will have enough people in your group to supervise your dog whilst you climb. Please let us know beforehand, as this affects where we will climb.

May I bring spectators?

On our private sessions spectators are welcome to join as long as the total group size (including spectators) doesn’t exceed 6. Please let us know if your planning on brining a spectator.
